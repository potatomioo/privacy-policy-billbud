Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Welcome to Split (also referred to as "Spliq", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our mobile application. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience and to facilitate bill splitting and group management, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up, we collect your phone number, Google profile information (name, email, and profile picture), and UID to create and authenticate your account.
  • Contacts List: With your explicit local permission, we access your device's contacts list. This is exclusively used to help you find friends, verify registered users (viral loop checking), and add members to your split groups. We do not store your entire contact list on our servers.
  • Financial & Expense Data: We store the structured details of the expenses you create, including amounts, group members involved, categories, and settlement statuses.
  • Device & Usage Information: We may collect standard diagnostic data such as device type, OS version, and crash reports to improve app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the essential functioning of the App:

  • To create and manage your secure account.
  • To match your local contacts with existing registered app users, displaying their registered names and avatars.
  • To calculate balances, debts, and synchronization of expense data across your groups utilizing Firebase Cloud Firestore.
  • To send necessary push notifications (e.g., when you owe money or are added to a group).
  • To improve the App's UI, UX, and squash bugs.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following scenarios:

  • With Other Users: When you are in a group, your group members will be able to see your Name, Profile Picture, and Shared Expenses.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services like Google Firebase for database hosting (Firestore) and Authentication. These providers are GDPR compliant and strictly process data on our behalf.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental authorities.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information as long as your account is active. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. To delete your account or specific data, please contact us directly, or use the in-app deletion tools (when available). Account deletion will anonymously preserve aggregate group expense data so other members' ledgers are not broken.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it, utilizing secure Google Firebase infrastructure. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at standard support channels.
